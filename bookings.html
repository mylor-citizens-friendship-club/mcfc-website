A bond of up to $500 is payable against loss, breakage, or damage to Club premises or equipment.

The bond covers damage caused by:

  • the hirer
  • the guests of the hirer
  • persons engaged by the hirer
  • equipment, electrical or otherwise used by them
  • Should the premises not be left in a clean condition, the cost of cleaning will be deducted from the bond.

A discount is available for Club Members and may also be negotiated for long-term bookings and local community "not for profit" functions.
