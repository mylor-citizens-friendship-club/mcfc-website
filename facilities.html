Hall Facilities

Carpeted Meeting/Reception Room

This multi-purpose room has gas heaters, ceiling fans, pool table (permission required), serving hatch from kitchen, access to kitchen and Main Hall. Approximate size: 8.2m x 7.5m

The Main Hall

The Main Hall has a well preserved floating dance floor, gas heaters, ceiling fans, raised stage with side dressing room, full stage with piano, floodlighting and excellent sound system both controlled from mixer units at rear of hall, access to kitchen, serving hatch from kitchen. Approximate size: 16m x 9m.

Stage Dimensions

Total stage width - 5.9m
Stage Opening width - 4.8m
Back of stage to curtain line - 4.1m
Back of stage to apron front - 4.7m
Ceiling height - 2.58m

Kitchen

Large, well equipped kitchen with six-burner gas stove, gas oven plus electric warming oven. Commercial dishwasher, large urn, pie warmer, microwave oven, commercial fridge and fridge/freezer, crockery, cutlery and glassware for up to 120 place settings, all inclusive in the Hall hire.

Mylor Hall is located on the corner of Strathalbyn Road and Cross Street, Mylor in the beautiful Adelaide hills.
